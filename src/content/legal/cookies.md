## What are cookies and local storage?

Cookies are small text files that a website can store on your device when you visit. They help a site remember information between pages and visits. **Local storage** is a related browser technology that lets a site save small amounts of data on your device, often for preferences. Together, these technologies help websites function and help owners understand how their sites are used. Some are essential; others are optional.

## Cookies this site uses

As a pre-launch marketing site, we keep our use of cookies minimal.

### Strictly necessary

These are required for the site to work correctly — for example, to load pages, remember basic preferences, or keep the site secure. The site cannot function properly without them, so they are always active and do not require consent.

### Analytics (may be added)

We may use analytics cookies or local storage to understand how visitors use the site in aggregate — such as which pages are viewed and how people arrive — so we can improve our content and messaging. These are optional. Where required by law, we will ask for your consent before setting non-essential analytics cookies, and we will update this policy to name the analytics provider we use before public launch.

## Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services we rely on — for example, an analytics provider or an email/newsletter tool used to manage early-access signups. These providers operate under their own privacy and cookie practices. We do not use cookies to sell your personal information. We will list the specific third-party services in the final, pre-launch version of this policy.

## Managing or disabling cookies

You are in control of cookies. Most browsers let you:

- View the cookies and local storage stored on your device.
- Delete existing cookies and stored data.
- Block all or some cookies, or be warned before cookies are set.

You can usually find these options in your browser's privacy or settings menu. Please note that blocking strictly necessary cookies may cause parts of the site to stop working as intended. Disabling optional analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the site.

If we offer a consent banner or preferences control, you can also use it to accept or decline optional cookies and change your choice later.
