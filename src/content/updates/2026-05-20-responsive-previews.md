---
title: "Product previews now adapt to their container"
description: "The in-page app previews respond to the space they're given, not just the screen size — so they stay legible everywhere."
date: "2026-05-20"
tag: "Site"
---

The recreated app surfaces on the site — the editor, dashboard, metadata, export, and audio previews — now respond to **their own container width** using container queries. A preview placed in a narrow column collapses to a compact layout instead of squeezing a desktop view, mirroring how the real studio adapts to width.
