# The Thirty Aethyrs

The Aethyrs are thirty concentric regions of the spirit world, named in the
angelic tongue and entered in descending order from TEX to LIL.

## Working the calls

Each Aethyr is opened with the eighteenth and nineteenth keys, substituting the
governing name of the Aethyr at the appointed place.
